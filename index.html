ABOUT ME

I graduated from UNC-Chapel Hill in May of 2019 with a B.S. in Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science. Over the past two years, I've become increasingly interested in building responsive and beautiful applications on the web and beyond. I try to use the technologies at my disposal together with my mathematical background to solve difficult problems in unconventional ways. I'm constantly exploring new ways of looking at the world around me, learning as much as I can as I go. I also still do some math stuff; talk to me anytime about applied category theory or topological data analyis.

When I'm not working, you can find me at the climbing gym or on some outdoor adventures. I'm also a routesetter at Progression Climbing and Fitness in Chapel Hill, NC. In an ideal world, I'd like to develop some software that would positively impact the climbing industry (ahem...looking at you, RockGymPro).
